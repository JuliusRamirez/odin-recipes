Salad

Desciption

People are subrised when they taste this salad with the odd combination of ingredients, but it's a very good salad.

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 2 heads fresh brocolli, florets only
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • ½ cup chopped green onions
  • 1 cup seedless green grapes
  • 1 cup seedless red grapes
  • ½ cup raisins
  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • ¼ cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Place bacon in large, deep skillet. Cook over medium heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble and set aside.

  2. Step 2

    In a large salad bowl, toss together the bacon, brocolli, celery, greem onions, green grapes, red grapes, raisins and almonds.

  3. Step 3

    Whisk together the mayonnaise, vinegar and sugar. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

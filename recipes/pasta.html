Pasta

Description

A flavorful homemade recipe for those who are tired of sweet pasta sauces

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage links
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) can canned tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can canned tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Removed casing from sausage links and cut into half inch slices. In a large skillet, brown sausage over medium heat for about 10 minuted; remove and set aside.

  2. Step 2

    In a large skillet, heat ground beef, olive oil, garlic and onion over medium heat until meat is nicely browned; drain.

  3. Step 3

    Pour in tomatoes and tomato sauc; mix in salt, ground black pepper, basil, oregano, bay leaf and cooked sausage. Simmer uncovered for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

  4. Step 4

    Bring in a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minuted or until a dente; drain.

  5. Step 5

    Mix cooked sauce with hot pasta and remove bay leaf from sauce before serving.
